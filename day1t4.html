Students who self-study gain the ability to choose what to study and how to study it according to their capabilities.

The confidence of a student is increased by self-study. It meets their requirement and enables unrestricted study.

Students can create their notes and study them in the language they are most familiar with using efficient self-study techniques.

Students who self-study have the freedom to learn at their speed and can concentrate on the subjects that interest them the most.

Self-learning helps to reduce the boredom, anxiety, or disappointment that students may experience in a classroom setting.

Self-learning gives students the freedom to select a subject they are passionate about learning, making for a more effective and engaging learning environment.
